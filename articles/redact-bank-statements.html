How to Redact Bank Statements Securely

Protect your financial privacy when sharing banking documents

Why Bank Statement Privacy Matters

Whether you're applying for a loan, verifying income for a rental, or submitting documents for insurance claims, you often need to share bank statements. But these documents contain highly sensitive information that could be used for fraud if it falls into the wrong hands.

⚠️ Information to ALWAYS Redact:

  • • Full account numbers (show only last 4 digits)
  • • Online banking passwords or PINs
  • • Unrelated transaction details
  • • Social Security Number
  • • Debit/Credit card numbers

What You Can Leave Visible

  • ✓ Your name and address (for verification)
  • ✓ Bank name and logo (shows authenticity)
  • ✓ Relevant deposits/income (highlight with boxes)
  • ✓ Statement period (shows recency)
