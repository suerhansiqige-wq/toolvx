Screenshot Privacy Protection: Essential Guide

Never expose sensitive information in screenshots again

The Hidden Dangers of Sharing Screenshots

Every day, millions of people share screenshots containing sensitive information: email addresses, phone numbers, account details, and even passwords. One careless post can lead to identity theft, phishing attacks, or unauthorized access to your accounts.

⚠️ Common Mistakes:

  • • Sharing error messages with account numbers visible
  • • Posting chat screenshots with phone numbers
  • • Sharing banking app screenshots with balances
  • • Posting work emails with colleague contact info

What to Always Redact from Screenshots

🔐 Account Information

  • • Email addresses
  • • Usernames
  • • Account numbers
  • • Phone numbers
  • • Physical addresses

💰 Financial Data

  • • Bank account numbers
  • • Credit card details
  • • Transaction amounts
  • • Balances
  • • Payment confirmations
