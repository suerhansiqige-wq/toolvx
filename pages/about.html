About toolvx

Free, local-first redaction for images and PDFs

Last updated: June 21, 2026

toolvx.com is an independent web project dedicated to helping people and organizations protect sensitive information in photos and PDF documents. We believe privacy tools should be free, fast, and trustworthy — without forcing you to upload confidential files to unknown servers.

Our Mission

We provide professional-grade image redaction and PDF sanitization that runs entirely in your browser. Faces, ID numbers, account details, signatures, and background identifiers can be obscured in seconds using mosaic pixelation, gaussian blur, or solid blackout — then exported in the original format. toolvx is a data sanitization tool, not a general photo or document editor.

Who We Are

toolvx is operated by a small independent team with backgrounds in web development and information security. We build and maintain the redaction engine, publish privacy guides on our blog, and respond to user feedback at admin@toolvx.com. We are transparent about how the site works: processing stays on your device, and we document advertising practices in our legal pages.

How We Are Different

  • 100% local processing — Your files never leave your device.
  • No account required — Open the page and start redacting immediately.
  • Multi-format support — JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and multi-page PDF.
  • Transparent operation — Clear privacy policy, contact email, and open documentation.

Who Uses toolvx

Journalists anonymizing sources, HR teams sanitizing internal screenshots, freelancers redacting client documents, parents blurring children's faces before social posts, and compliance officers preparing shareable PDF copies — all rely on local redaction to minimize data exposure.

Editorial & Content Standards

Our blog publishes original articles about image privacy, GDPR-aligned practices, PDF redaction workflows, and secure sharing habits. Each guide is written for real users facing real privacy decisions. We cite practical steps, compare redaction techniques honestly, and update articles when regulations or browser capabilities change. We do not publish auto-generated filler or duplicate pages targeting minor keyword variations.

Read our latest guides on the Blog, or start with How to Redact Faces in Photos and PDF Redaction Tools Compared.

Advertising & Sustainability

toolvx is free to use. We display Google AdSense advertisements to support hosting and development costs. Ads are clearly separated from the redaction workspace and never interfere with file processing. Advertising cookies load only after you accept our cookie banner. See our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy for details.

Contact the Team

Questions, feedback, or partnership inquiries: admin@toolvx.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours for general requests. Visit our Contact page for more options.
