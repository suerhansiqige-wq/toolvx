Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can both make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

At toolvx.com, we use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference or cookie consent choice) to provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Advertising Cookies: We use Google AdSense to display ads. These cookies help serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. Advertising cookies are set only if you click Accept on our cookie consent banner. We do not use Google Analytics or similar analytics services.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Enable website functionality during your visit Deleted when you close your browser
Language Preference Remember your selected language 1 year
Consent Preference Store your cookie consent choice (Accept or Decline) 1 year
Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense) Serve and measure ads; may include cookies such as the DoubleClick cookie Up to 13 months (Google-controlled)

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. When you first visit toolvx.com, our cookie consent banner lets you Accept or Decline advertising cookies. You can also exercise your preferences by modifying your browser settings. Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Our image and PDF redaction tools run locally in your browser — your files are not uploaded to our servers. However, we partner with Google AdSense to display advertisements that help keep toolvx.com free.

Consent-first loading: Google AdSense scripts and advertising cookies load only after you click Accept on our cookie consent banner. If you click Decline, AdSense is not initialized and no advertising cookies are set by us for that purpose.

When ads are shown, Google may set third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other websites. Google is responsible for its own cookies and privacy practices. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business operations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
