Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our privacy-focused redaction tool

Is this tool really free?

Yes! Our tool is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or usage limits. You can redact unlimited images and PDFs without paying anything.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your images and PDFs never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I use this tool offline?

Yes! Once the page has loaded, you can use all features without an internet connection. Perfect for working on sensitive documents in secure environments.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP images, and PDF files. Multi-page PDFs are fully supported with page-by-page editing capabilities.

How do I blur faces or hide text?

Simply upload your image, click and drag to select the area you want to redact, choose your preferred color or effect, adjust intensity if needed, and export the result.

Will exported files keep the original filename?

Yes! We preserve the exact original filename, including all characters (Chinese, Japanese, Korean, emojis, etc.). No more UUID or "generated" filenames.

Can I edit multiple pages in a PDF?

Absolutely! Our tool supports multi-page PDFs. You can navigate between pages, edit each one individually, and export as a complete multi-page PDF.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our responsive design works perfectly on smartphones and tablets (iOS and Android). Touch-friendly interface makes it easy to select areas on smaller screens.
